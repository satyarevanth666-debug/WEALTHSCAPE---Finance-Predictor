What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly (e.g., monthly) in mutual funds. It builds discipline and averages out market volatility.

The Power of Compounding

Compounding is earning returns on both your original investment and past returns. Albert Einstein called compound interest the 8th wonder of the world.

Understanding Inflation

Inflation is the increase in prices over time which reduces purchasing power. Investing helps your money grow faster than inflation.

Why Start Early?

Time is the most important factor in investing. Starting early allows your money more time to grow through compounding.
